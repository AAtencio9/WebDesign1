Fun Facts About Football

  • Football is pretty young, only being 139 years old
  • The NFL hired its first full-time female official in 2015
  • American Football evolved from soccer and rugby
  • The Miami Dolphins are the only team to have a complete perfect season
  • in 1916, Georgia Tech scored the most lopsided victory in football history - 222-0.
  • Every Team except for the Texans have played in a conference championship since 1988
  • The Packers won the first superbowl ever (and the second one)
  • Originally, touchdowns were worth 4 points, while field goals were worth 5
  • The Forward pass was originally illegal
  • The huddle was first used in the 1890s by quarterback Paul Hubbard, who was deaf. Hubbard was concerned that the other team could interpret his hand signlas, so he brought his teammates into a round formation to call plays
