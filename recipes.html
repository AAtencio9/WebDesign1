HOT & SPICY BAKED BUFFALO CHICKEN WINGS

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken wings (cut)
  • 1 cup chicken broth or water
  • 3/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Cooking Spray for the baking sheet

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 450 degrees F. Coat a rimmed baking sheet with cooking spray or lightly brush with oil
  2. To deep pan or skillet; add chicen broth, hot sauce, paprika, and butter, bring to a simmer over medium heat. Add the chicken and simmer for 15 minutes. Transfer the wings to the prepared baking sheet and arrange in a single layer. Reserve the sauce in the pan.
  3. Bake the wings in the upper rack, turn once after 15 minutes then allow to bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the skin is golden brown. Meanwhile, simmer the reseved sauce over medium-low head until slightly thickened, about 10-12 minutes.
  4. Return the wings to the skillet and toss with the remaining sauce. Serve the wings with blue cheese dip and celery.
